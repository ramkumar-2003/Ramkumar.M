IoT Based Spirometer

The healthcare landscape is seeing a revolution with the introduction of IoT-based spirometer. These innovative devices measure lung function by recording breathing patterns, but with a modern twist. The Spirometer is used to detect the disease like COPD, Asthma can be use of spirogram. Since the patients may suffer certain breathing problems after their surgery it also helps them in overcoming those issues. This allows them to wirelessly transmit data to smartphones or cloud platforms. This not only enhances convenience but also unlocks further functionalities. Cloud-based analysis can provide real-time feedback and track trends over time, empowering patients and healthcare professionals with a more comprehensive picture of lung health.

IoT Based Device For Skin Disease Identification

Leveraging the power of IoT, new skin disease identification devices are transforming healthcare. These compact systems capture close-up images of skin concerns and securely transmit them to the cloud. There, machine learning algorithms analyze the data against a vast database of known diseases, generating potential matches with confidence scores. Delivered through a user-friendly app, this information empowers individuals to monitor their skin health and potentially achieve earlier detection of concerning conditions. This technology is particularly beneficial for those in remote areas or with limited mobility, improving access to diagnosis and self-monitoring.
